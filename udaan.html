Udaan - The Euphoric leap


Role

Udaan 2016 - Team member   |   Udaan 2017 - Head Developer

Details

Udaan is a 3 day long Cultural fest organized by our College, where more than 5000 students come together and compete each other showcasing their varied skills in Dancing, Singing, Puzzle solving, Coding and many more.

Looking at the huge participation in numerous events, Developers team was devised for technical support. This proved as a perfect opportunity for developers like me to contribute to open source by building a robust and reusable code using latest technologies without any interference.

Along with many application I developed custom styled, responsive and lightweight websites according to themes decided.
