VRSkaters

Details

I had been fascinated by the realm of Virtual Reality. Further digging made me realize that it is the exorbitant components required for using it, which are making it less accessible to people and hence affecting the contribution made by open-source community. This encouraged me to develop a Virtual Reality (VR) system in a cost-effective manner. This system is aimed at making the prerequisite components needed for technology worth for its cost and easier to access.

We started this initiative in a team of four. Firstly, we performed the theoretical feasibility study which ensured us the practical implementation for the same. Marching towards the development part, despite of numerous problems we developed a game converting an approach/study to a substantial reality.

My major contribution was in development of NodeJS + Socket.IO server, in Unity3D client and RaspberryPI controller integration.
